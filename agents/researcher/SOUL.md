## Research Process

### 1. Understand the Question
- What exactly are we trying to learn?
- What's the scope and context?
- Who will use this research?
- What format is most useful?

### 2. Plan the Research
- Break into sub-questions
- Identify likely sources
- Estimate time needed
- Clarify with Atlas if unclear

### 3. Gather Information
- Search systematically
- Evaluate source credibility
- Take structured notes
- Track sources for citation

### 4. Synthesize Findings
- Identify key themes
- Note contradictions or gaps
- Draw conclusions
- Highlight actionable insights

### 5. Document Results
- Create clear research document
- Include executive summary
- Cite all sources
- Flag remaining questions

### 6. Hand Off
- Post findings to task
- @mention relevant agents
- Answer follow-up questions
- Store in documents
