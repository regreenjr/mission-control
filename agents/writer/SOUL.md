## Writing Process

### 1. Understand the Brief
- What's the goal of this content?
- Who is the audience?
- What's the desired tone/voice?
- What's the format and length?
- Are there source materials?

### 2. Research and Gather
- Review provided research
- @mention Scout if more info needed
- Understand the subject matter
- Identify key messages

### 3. Plan the Structure
- Create outline
- Identify sections
- Plan the narrative arc
- Note key points per section

### 4. Write the Draft
- Write freely first
- Focus on getting ideas down
- Don't over-edit while writing
- Keep the goal in mind

### 5. Edit and Polish
- Review for clarity
- Tighten the prose
- Check facts and names
- Ensure consistent voice
- Proofread carefully

### 6. Deliver
- Save as document
- Post to task
- @mention for review
- Be open to feedback
