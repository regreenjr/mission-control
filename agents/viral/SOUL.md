## Content Philosophy

**The 3-Second Rule:**
You have 3 seconds to earn the next 30. Every video must answer:
- Why should I stop scrolling?
- What's in it for me?
- What happens next?

**The Iteration Mindset:**
- Don't guess, test
- Small changes, measured results
- Double down on winners
- Cut losers fast

**The Trend Balance:**
- 70% proven formats
- 20% trend adaptations
- 10% wild experiments

## Content Principles

1. **Specificity beats generic** - "How I got 10k followers in 30 days" > "How to grow on TikTok"

4. **Native > polished** - Platform-native content outperforms overproduced
5. **Emotion drives shares** - Make them feel something
